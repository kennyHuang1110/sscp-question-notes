## Topic 7

### Question 1

In computing what is the name of a non-self-replicating type of malware program containing malicious code that appears to have some useful purpose but also contains code that has a malicious or harmful purpose imbedded in it, when executed, carries out actions that are unknown to the person installing it, typically causing loss or theft of data, and possible system harm.

**Choices:**

- A. virus
- B. worm
- C. Trojan horse.
- D. trapdoor

**Answer:** C. Trojan horse.

### Question 2

Which of the following virus types changes some of its characteristics as it spreads?

**Choices:**

- A. Boot Sector
- B. Parasitic
- C. Stealth
- D. Polymorphic

**Answer:** D. Polymorphic

### Question 3

Virus scanning and content inspection of SMIME encrypted e-mail without doing any further processing is:

**Choices:**

- A. Not possible
- B. Only possible with key recovery scheme of all user keys
- C. It is possible only if X509 Version 3 certificates are used
- D. It is possible only by "brute force" decryption

**Answer:** A. Not possible

### Question 4

Which virus category has the capability of changing its own code, making it harder to detect by anti-virus software?

**Choices:**

- A. Stealth viruses
- B. Polymorphic viruses
- C. Trojan horses
- D. Logic bombs

**Answer:** B. Polymorphic viruses

### Question 5

The high availability of multiple all-inclusive, easy-to-use hacking tools that do NOT require much technical knowledge has brought a growth in the number of which type of attackers?

**Choices:**

- A. Black hats
- B. White hats
- C. Script kiddies
- D. Phreakers

**Answer:** C. Script kiddies

### Question 6

Which of the following computer crime is MORE often associated with INSIDERS?

**Choices:**

- A. IP spoofing
- B. Password sniffing
- C. Data diddling
- D. Denial of service (DOS)

**Answer:** C. Data diddling

### Question 7

What do the ILOVEYOU and Melissa virus attacks have in common?

**Choices:**

- A. They are both denial-of-service (DOS) attacks.
- B. They have nothing in common.
- C. They are both masquerading attacks.
- D. They are both social engineering attacks.

**Answer:** C. They are both masquerading attacks.

### Question 8

Crackers today are MOST often motivated by their desire to:

**Choices:**

- A. Help the community in securing their networks.
- B. Seeing how far their skills will take them.
- C. Getting recognition for their actions.
- D. Gaining Money or Financial Gains.

**Answer:** D. Gaining Money or Financial Gains.

### Question 9

What best describes a scenario when an employee has been shaving off pennies from multiple accounts and depositing the funds into his own bank account?

**Choices:**

- A. Data fiddling
- B. Data diddling
- C. Salami techniques
- D. Trojan horses

**Answer:** C. Salami techniques

### Question 10

Java is not:

### Question 11

**Choices:**
